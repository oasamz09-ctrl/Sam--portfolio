Stakeholder Analysis

MySanity AI Behavioural Intelligence Platform

Identification and analysis of all key stakeholders involved in the design, development, and delivery of the MySanity AI ecosystem.

Overview

MySanity is a multi-layered AI system combining behavioural data (mood, sleep, activity) with conversational AI (text + voice).

This creates a complex stakeholder ecosystem involving technical teams, business roles, and end users interacting with the platform.

Key Stakeholders

End Users

Primary users who log mood, track sleep, and interact with the AI assistant.

Product Owner

Owns the product vision, defines priorities, and aligns features with business goals.

Business Analyst (You)

Translates business needs into structured requirements, user stories, and system workflows.

AI Engineers

Build conversational AI (text + voice) and behavioural insight algorithms.

Data Engineers

Manage ingestion and processing of mood, sleep, and behavioural datasets.

Backend Developers

Handle APIs, authentication, and system integration across services.

Mobile Developers

Build user-facing application and ensure smooth AI interaction experience.

UX/UI Designers

Design user journeys, interfaces, and interaction flows.

Influence vs Interest Matrix

  • High Influence / High Interest: Product Owner, AI Engineers
  • High Influence / Low Interest: Backend Developers, Data Engineers
  • Low Influence / High Interest: End Users
  • Low Influence / Low Interest: External observers
