Stakeholder Analysis

MySanity AI Behavioural Intelligence Platform

Identification and analysis of key stakeholders involved in the design, development, and delivery of a proactive AI-powered mental health ecosystem.

Overview

MySanity is an AI-driven mental health platform designed to shift users from reactive support to proactive behavioural awareness using mood tracking, sleep data, journaling, and conversational AI (text + voice).

The system requires coordination across multiple technical, business, and user-facing stakeholders to ensure safe, scalable, and meaningful mental health insights.

Key Stakeholders

End Users

Individuals using the platform to track mood, sleep, and interact with AI for proactive mental wellbeing insights.

Product Owner

Defines product vision, prioritises features, and ensures alignment with business and user outcomes.

Business Analyst (You)

Translates behavioural and business needs into structured requirements, user journeys, and system workflows.

AI / ML Engineers

Develop conversational AI (text + voice) and behavioural insight models that power personalised recommendations.

Data Engineers

Build pipelines that collect, clean, and structure mood, sleep, and journaling data for analysis.

Backend Developers

Design APIs, authentication, and system infrastructure supporting secure and scalable data flow.

Mobile Developers

Deliver user-facing application experiences including mood tracking, chat interface, and dashboards.

UX/UI Designers

Design intuitive mental health journeys ensuring low cognitive load and high emotional safety.

Stakeholder Prioritisation (Impact View)

  • High Influence / High Impact: Product Owner, AI Engineers, Backend Architects
  • High Influence / Medium Impact: Data Engineers, UX/UI Designers
  • Medium Influence / High Impact: Business Analyst, Mobile Developers
  • High Importance / Low Influence: End Users (core beneficiaries of the system)
