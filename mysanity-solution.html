MySanity — Solution & UI Showcase

AI Behavioural Intelligence for Proactive Mental Wellbeing

This section presents the final interface design of MySanity, focusing on low cognitive load, proactive mental health support, and unified wellbeing tracking through AI-driven insights.

Solution Overview

MySanity is a proactive mental wellbeing platform that uses behavioural data to detect early signs of stress and provide personalised interventions before issues escalate into crises.

  • AI-powered mood and behaviour tracking
  • Unified wellbeing dashboard
  • Low-effort daily check-ins
  • Proactive stress detection and intervention
  • Emotionally safe AI guidance

Key Features

1. AI Mood Intelligence

The system analyses behavioural signals such as activity patterns, sleep, and engagement trends to infer emotional states and detect early stress indicators.

2. Daily Check-In (Low Effort Input)

Users provide quick, single-step emotional check-ins designed to reduce cognitive load and improve consistency.

3. Proactive Interventions

Instead of waiting for user input, MySanity surfaces timely recommendations when early signs of stress are detected.

4. Unified Wellbeing Dashboard

A single view combining mood trends, behavioural insights, and AI suggestions to reduce fragmentation across apps.

User Flow

The experience is designed to reduce friction and guide users from awareness to action in minimal steps.

  • 1. Open app → View AI wellbeing summary
  • 2. Quick mood check-in (optional)
  • 3. AI analyses behavioural signals
  • 4. Proactive suggestions delivered if needed
  • 5. User engages with recommended intervention

Design Principles

  • Reduce cognitive load at every interaction
  • Prioritise emotional safety and tone sensitivity
  • Make wellbeing tracking effortless and consistent
  • Shift from reactive to proactive mental health support
  • Unify fragmented wellbeing experiences into one system
