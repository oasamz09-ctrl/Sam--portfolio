MySanity — Solution & UI Showcase

AI Behavioural Intelligence for Proactive Mental Wellbeing

This section presents the final interface design of MySanity, showing how research insights were translated into a low-friction, proactive mental wellbeing experience.

From Problem to Solution

Users often delay mental health support until stress escalates into a crisis, while existing tools remain reactive. MySanity addresses this by shifting towards proactive, behaviour-driven intervention that detects early signs of stress and responds before issues escalate.

Solution Overview

MySanity is a proactive mental wellbeing platform that uses behavioural intelligence to detect early emotional signals and deliver personalised support in real time.

  • AI-powered mood and behavioural tracking
  • Unified wellbeing dashboard
  • Low-effort daily check-ins
  • Proactive stress detection and intervention
  • Emotionally safe AI communication

Key Features

AI Mood Intelligence

Interprets behavioural signals such as sleep, activity, and engagement patterns to identify early emotional shifts.

Low-Effort Check-Ins

Simple one-tap input system designed to reduce cognitive load and encourage consistency.

Proactive Interventions

Instead of waiting for user input, the system delivers timely recommendations when risk patterns are detected.

Unified Wellbeing Dashboard

Combines mood trends, behavioural insights, and AI suggestions into a single cohesive view.

Design Rationale

  • Reduced cognitive load through minimal interaction steps
  • Designed for emotional safety and non-judgemental tone
  • Prioritised proactive intervention over reactive support
  • Unified fragmented wellbeing tools into one system
  • Optimised for mobile-first, high-frequency usage

User Flow

  • Open app → View AI wellbeing summary
  • Optional quick mood check-in
  • Behavioural signals analysed in real time
  • Proactive suggestions triggered if needed
  • User engages with personalised intervention
