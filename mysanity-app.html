MySanity

AI Behavioural Intelligence Platform

A Business Analysis case study exploring how mood tracking, sleep data, and conversational AI (text + voice) can be integrated into a unified wellbeing system.

Stakeholder Analysis Requirements

Overview

MySanity is a conceptual AI-powered wellbeing platform designed to consolidate fragmented behavioural data (mood, sleep, activity) into a single intelligent system.

The goal is to generate meaningful insights that help users understand patterns in their wellbeing and improve long-term behavioural outcomes.

The platform also introduces conversational AI (text + voice) to support natural interaction and real-time guidance.

Business Problem

  • Wellbeing data is currently fragmented across multiple apps
  • Users lack meaningful insight into behavioural patterns
  • Existing tools provide generic recommendations with low personalisation
  • Engagement drops due to lack of integrated experience

My Role (Business Analyst)

  • Defined product requirements and system scope
  • Designed user journeys and behavioural flows
  • Created stakeholder structure and system mapping
  • Translated business needs into functional requirements
  • Supported MVP feature prioritisation and planning
