MySanity

AI Behavioural Intelligence & Mental Health Platform

A Business Analysis case study exploring how fragmented mental health data can be unified into a proactive AI-powered wellbeing system combining mood tracking, sleep analysis, journaling, and conversational AI (text + voice).

Stakeholder Analysis Requirements

Overview

MySanity is a conceptual AI-powered mental health platform designed to shift users from reactive wellbeing management to proactive behavioural awareness.

It consolidates fragmented data sources such as mood tracking, sleep patterns, journaling, and AI conversations into a single behavioural intelligence system.

The system uses AI to generate personalised insights that help users identify patterns, understand emotional triggers, and improve long-term mental wellbeing.

Business Problem

  • Mental health data is fragmented across multiple standalone applications
  • Users lack visibility of long-term behavioural and emotional patterns
  • Existing wellbeing tools provide generic insights with limited personalisation
  • Users engage with mental health apps reactively rather than proactively
  • There is no unified system combining mood, sleep, journaling, and AI interaction into one behavioural model

My Role (Business Analyst)

  • Defined end-to-end business problem and product vision
  • Conducted root cause analysis of fragmented mental health systems
  • Translated business needs into structured MVP requirements
  • Designed user journeys and behavioural feedback loops
  • Defined stakeholder structure and system dependencies
  • Supported AI product thinking for conversational (text + voice) support
